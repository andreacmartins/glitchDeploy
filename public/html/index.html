A Beginner Full Stack Web Development Tutorial

This is a ugly website right? That´s because it´s a tutorial being made by a beginner, for beginners. I want to teach myself how to do web development for personal projects, and i think that building web tutorial kills two birds with one stone, since i´m deploying something, beginning with the easy steps, and documenting all the way so that, by explaining, i can really understand what i´m doing.

So, at first, whoever chooses to follow this tutorial will begin building the ugliest and non-responsive of websites, but deployed on the internet (not so easy as it seems) all to the way to where i want to get, that is, know the basics of enough tools to develop a "MVP-like" web app with authentication, payments that at least not ugly. I believe this to be a great foundation to move on to the more advanced topics that will be necessary for the use case you are interested in.

The ultimate goal to is to learn by doing small steps that are as simple as possible and experimenting, in a fun and tactile way. Just like kids learn, and kids are great learners, aren´t they?

No experience needed, just knowing how to use a computer (word, excel) and basic math and english.

If that sounds good to you, keep going
